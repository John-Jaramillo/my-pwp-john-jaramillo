About Me

I am a Fullstack Web Developer in Albuquerque, NM. I'm currently finishing up the Deep Dive Fullstack Web Development bootcamp at CNMIngenuity and will be taking the Java + Android bootcamp to further my coding skills. This is my first time I've gotten immersed in coding and I've found that I love it! Despite the feeling of drowning under a deluge of infomation and the frustration of debugging because of a lost semicolon, there is nothing like the feeling you get when it finally works.

I've spent many years doing other jobs including working with mentally handicapped and autistic individuals (both in home based and institutional based settings), manufacturing injectable drugs, computer tech support, and call center work. Since I was laid off during the Covid 19 pandemic, it was the perfect time to attend the Deepdive Bootcamps. Although my past experience hasn't been in coding, I've picked up other valuable skills along the way that will allow me to become a better coder and employee. Manufacturing drugs is a field where one mistake can contaminate a whole batch of medication. This stressed to me the importance of attention to detail, following established protocols, and teamwork. From working with the mentally handicapped, I learned the importance of patience, humor, and putting things into perspective. Appreciating what you have and enjoying your work and work environment is much more important than a bigger paycheck doing something you hate.
