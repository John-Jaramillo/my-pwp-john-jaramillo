Content Strategy

My website will be one page and will contain the following three sections:

About Me / Skills: This section give my name and will outline my experience and professional skills.

Portfolio: This section will be an image gallery of a few samples of my work along with a short discription. Clicking a sample will give a more in depth description of the project.

Contact Me: This section will contain a contact form along with info on my e-mail address and LinkedIn and GitHub profiles.
